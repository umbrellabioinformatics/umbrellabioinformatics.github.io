About Us

This is a virtual laboratory without affiliation to any particular institution, in which members conduct research, using computational and theoretical methods of biophysics to understand nano and large biomolecular complex, with the purpose of developing drugs, vaccines and understanding molecular mechanisms that underlie them at the atomic level with our computational microscope, as Prof. Klaus Schulten had already related, from which we were inspired.
